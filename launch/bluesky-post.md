## Post 2

The pitch in one line:

A format for typed hypergraphs of entities + relations + events + intentions, anchored in space and time, renderable by any engine, authored by any LLM.

v1.0 extracted from what we ship in production (Portals AR app). Not committee-designed.

## Post 3

What it composes with (not replaces):

- glTF → assets
- USD → scenes-for-rendering
- USDZ → Apple's capture-play
- XRAI → the graph layer above, with agent-memory semantics

One format. Infinite uses: scene graph, episodic memory, living knowledge base.
